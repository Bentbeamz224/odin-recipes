Chicken Alfredo

Description

Perfectly cooked noodles with flavorful chicken, coated with creamy Alfredo sauce

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (1/2 pound) fettuccine noodles
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 3/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 2-3 cloves of Garlic
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Pinch of ground nutmeg

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta:
    • Bring water to a boil: Bring water to a rolling boil and cook the fettuccine according to package instructions until it's al dente.
    • Reserve Water Before draining pasta, scoop out 1/2 cup of the pasta water and set aside, then drain pasta.
  2. Cook the Chicken:
    • Season:
      • Pat 1 pound of chicken breasts dry and season them generously with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
    • Sear:
      • Heat 1 tbsp of olive oil & 1 tbsp of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook for 5 to 7 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.
    • Rest & Slice:
      • Remove the chicken from the skillet to a cutting board, let it rest for 3 minutes, and slice into strips.
  3. Make the Alfredo Sauce:
    • Melt & Saute: In the same skillet (to preserve flavor), melt 4 tbsp of butter over medium heat. Add 2-3 cloves of minced garlics amd saute for 30 to 60 seconds until fragrant.
    • Simmer & Thicken: Pour in 1 cup of heavy cream. Bring the cream to a gentle simmer and let it cook for 3 to 4 minutes until its slightly thickens.
    • Add Cheese Remove the skillet from heat, then whisk in 1 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese and a pinch of ground nutmeg until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  4. Combine & Serve:
    • Toss: Add your cooked fettuccine directly into the sauce. Toss well to coat the noodles. If the sauce is too thick, strin in a splash of your reserved pasta water.
    • Top & Serve: Add the sliced chicken into the pasta (or lay it on top) and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
