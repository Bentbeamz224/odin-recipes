Sloppy Joe

Description

Perfectly cooked and juicy beef mixed with a flavorful sauce and served as a sandwich

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (85% or 90% lean)
  • 1/2 medium yellow or sweet onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Tbsp cooking oil
  • 8 oz can tomato sauce
    • <1i>1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1-2 Tbsp brown sugar, to taste
  • 1 to 1 1/2 tsp yellow mustard
  • Hamburger buns of your choosing
  • (Optional)Sliced cheese

Cooking Instructions

  1. Saute the aromatics: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add your chopped onion and bell pepper. Cook for about 5 minutes, or until softened, then add your garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  2. Brown the beef: Add the ground beef to the skillet. Break the meat into crumbles, and cook until fully browned. Tilt the pan and spoon out excess grease.
  3. Simmer the sauce: Stir in the ketchup. Worcestershire sauce, yellow mustard, brown sugar, and tomato sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Thicken: Bring the mixture to a light boil, then reduce heat to low. Simmer uncovered for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce is thick and rich.
  5. Serve: Spoon the hot mixture generously onto toasted Hamburger buns and serve hot.
